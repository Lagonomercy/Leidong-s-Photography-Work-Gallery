Street Fragments

A small street photography project by Leidong Zhao, documenting different corners of Southern California. Each series focuses on a different city and its rhythm: coastal streets, dense downtown blocks, and quiet suburban lines.

Explore the series

  • Oxnard – Coastal Streets – low-rise buildings, wide roads, and everyday moments near the ocean.
  • DTLA – Urban Motion – reflections, headlights, and crowded crossings in downtown Los Angeles.
  • Irvine – Suburban Stillness – geometric streets, quiet parks, and structured calm.

Built and deployed by myself using Hugo, GitHub, and Netlify as part of my personal portfolio.
